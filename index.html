Our Product/Service

Phage therapy is a personalized treatment that uses viruses (called phages) to specifically target and kill harmful bacteria. Unlike antibiotics, phages only attack the bacteria they are designed to target, leaving healthy cells unharmed.

The benefits of using phage therapy for treating bacterial infections include:

  • Effective targeting of specific bacteria
  • Reduced risk of antibiotic resistance
  • Personalized treatment plans
  • Fewer side effects
